What is the Idle Arcade?

Idle Arcade is an interactive clicker game that allows users to play and relax on their leisure time. The game consists four parts, the main clicker game, mini-games, a shop and a leaderboard. The goal is to collect as many as possible Arcade Tickets (ATs) and these tickets can be used to purchases upgrades and helpers from the shop. After login, users are directed to the main game and are able to click on the arcade machine to collect as many game tickets as they can. The more they click, the more tickets they gain. Users are also able to purchase upgrades and helpers which will increase the Arcade Tickets gained per second. Aside from this, the various independent mini-games are provided as bonus games to allow users to accumulate even more Arcade Tickets. All users' scores will be ranked and displayed on the leaderboard. You can also report any issues to us by clicking "Bug Report" on the main page!

How are Tickets calculated?
Tickets are calculated based on points earned from clicking game, points earned from mini-games, and any upgrades/helpers you have.

Where to find my score?
Learderboard is available to allow you check up-to-date all user ranking. You can also see your total score on the main screen.

How are Tickets calculated?
Tickets are calculated based on points earned from clicking game + points earned from mini-games.

Leaderboard is available here to allow you check up-to-date all user rankings.

What is shop used for?
You can purchase Helpers / Upgrade to help accelerate clicking rate! Thus you will be able to collect more tickets with faster rate.
